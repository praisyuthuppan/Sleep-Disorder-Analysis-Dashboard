# Sleep-Disorder-Analysis-Dashboard
Developed an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze and compare two common sleep disorders – Insomnia and Sleep Apnea – using a sleep health dataset.
 The dashboard focuses on key factors such as sleep duration, sleep quality, stress levels, BMI, physical activity, age, gender, and occupation to uncover meaningful patterns in sleep behavior.

📊 Using DAX measures and KPI indicators, I performed a detailed comparison between both conditions and derived valuable insights.

💡 Key Insights:
- Insomnia is primarily driven by psychological and lifestyle factors, with individuals showing higher stress levels, lower sleep quality, and shorter sleep duration, whereas sleep apnea is more associated with physical health conditions such as higher BMI and increasing age.
- Age and gender patterns indicate that insomnia affects all age groups, while sleep apnea is more prominent among middle-aged and older individuals, and both disorders show higher prevalence among females based on filter analysis.
- Sleep quality vs stress analysis shows a strong negative relationship in insomnia, while in sleep apnea, sleep disturbances are mainly caused by breathing interruptions rather than stress factors.
-  Occupation and activity patterns reveal that insomnia is more common in high-stress or sedentary roles (such as office workers and students), whereas sleep apnea is less occupation-dependent but more linked to lower physical activity levels and overall health conditions.
  
🎯 This project helped me strengthen my skills in data analysis, data modeling, and dashboard design using Power BI while transforming raw data into actionable insights.
